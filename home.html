Need help with lubricant oils?

Synthetic oil is chemically engineered for better performance, higher temperature resistance, and longer life. Mineral oil is derived from refined crude oil and is typically cheaper, but may require more frequent changes.

The oil change interval depends on the type of oil and the manufacturer's recommendation. Generally, synthetic oils last longer (around 7,500-10,000 miles), while conventional oils may need to be changed every 3,000-5,000 miles.
